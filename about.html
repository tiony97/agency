Who we are

We are a licensed land base manpower and placement agency duly certified by the KENYAN GOVERNMENT (NEA). We offer our services by providing competent, qualified and good-natured workers to foreign principals for overseas job placement through known methodology of professionalized recruitment and skill selection.

We offer skilled and non skilled jobs. For the process of recruitment and travelling it mainly depends with the type of job. Ropasil Recruitment Agency was founded in 2018 and is registered under NEA Our founder/CEO is Miss Roseline Wanjiku.

We have a branch in Saudi and willing to expand to other countries. Our value is Quality Recruitment Services and our mission is to extend empathy and lift everyone to their utmost potential. Our goal is to be the best Agency full of trust and confidence from our Clients.

Ropasil's Vision

Our goal is to provide competent, qualified and good-natured workers to foreign principals for overseas job placement through known methodology of professionalized recruitment and skill selection. Ropasil's core value is Quality Recruitment Services and our mission is to extend empathy and lift everyone to their utmost potential.

According to the Kenya National Bureau of Statistics (KNBS), the Arab League has in the past decade outpaced the USA and Europe as Kenya's biggest job export market, pushing up diaspora remittance from the region. The recent Covid-19 pandemic has rendered many people jobless, pushing up unemployment rate to a high of 15 per cent. Soft skills training to drive self-employment and job export offer a possible remedy to this problem. A remedy which Ropasil aims to tackle head on by ensuring its applicants recenive the best training to ensure their success at work.

We aim to be the best Agency full of trust and confidence from our Clients.
